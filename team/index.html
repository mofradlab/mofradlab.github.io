Team

The lab brings together graduate students, postdocs, and undergraduate researchers working at the intersection of computational mechanics and cell biology.

Fall 2023 Lab Picture!

Principal Investigator

Dr. Mohammad R.K. Mofrad

Professor

Cell & Nuclear Biomechanics Statistical Learning & Biological Language Processing Microbiome & Bacterial Community Biomechanics

Prof. Mofrad leads the Molecular Cell Biomechanics Laboratory, pursuing a multidisciplinary research program at the interface of biomechanics, mechanobiology, artificial intelligence, and computational biology. The laboratory investigates how physical forces shape biological structure and function across molecular, cellular, and multicellular scales.
